Live Testing & QA Manual

Step-by-step verification guide for DevNexus Platform operations.

1. Staging Verification Overview

This document details the exact sequence of workflow steps required to test and evaluate the B2B client and developer workspaces, AI requirements generator, and secure signaling channels.

2. Phase 1: Authentication & Role Setup

Verify role isolation and metadata settings inside the Supabase database system.

Steps to register:
  1. Open the live website URL in your browser.
  2. Click Create Account at the bottom of the sign-in block.
  3. Select Client / Founder to register a business client, or Vetted Developer for developer profiles.
  4. Fill in the specific details and click Create Secured Account.
  5. Verify that you successfully redirect to the platform and can log out.

3. Phase 2: AI Requirement Vetting

Test the advisory hub natural language processing and PostgreSQL insertions.

Steps to verify:
  1. Select AI Advisory Hub from the left sidebar navigation menu.
  2. Type in a requirement brief (e.g. "Secure WebRTC deal room with file locker encryption keys.").
  3. Select skill tags and click Submit to AI Vetting Engine.
  4. Verify the generated solution summary blocks (Roadmap, Cost, Risks).

4. Phase 3: Developer Registry Matching

Ensure that developers can be selected from the graph view directory.

Steps to verify:
  1. Go to Developer Registry.
  2. Click on skill tags to filter the registry profiles list.
  3. Click on a profile (e.g. Alex Rivers) and click Open Secure Deal Room.

5. Phase 4: Secure NDA & Signature Vetting

Sign the mutual agreement contract on the digital drawing pad.

Steps to sign:
  1. Customize the NDA parameters in the Setup tab.
  2. Copy the generated One-Time Access Key (used by the peer to join).
  3. Draw your digital signature on the drawing canvas.
  4. Click Confirm & Secure Signature and wait for the room to activate.

6. Phase 5: Real-Time Deal Room Sync & Wipe

Test real-time message relays and secure room scrubbing.

Steps to verify:
  1. Open a second Private/Incognito window and log in.
  2. Select the Private Deal Room tab, enter the Access Key, and click Join.
  3. Send chat messages and verify they update in real time.
  4. Click Close & Wipe Session in either window and verify the data-scrubbing sweeping screen and SHA-256 audit hash logs.
